Mahjong麻將májiàng

Hong Kong ruleset · 136 tiles · 4 players A one-page reference for beginners (standard hand only). GoalBe the first to assemble a winning hand — 4 melds + 1 pair (14 tiles) — by drawing tiles or claiming others' discards.
01 · Tiles The deck牌pái 34 unique designs × 4 copies = 136 tiles. Three numbered suits 1–9, plus four winds and three dragons (the "honor" tiles).
萬Characters Wàn — "ten thousand". Numbered 1–9.
條Bamboo Tiáo — sticks. The 1-bamboo is a bird.
餅Dots Bǐng — coins. Numbered 1–9.
風 · 箭 Honors Winds · Dragons / Fēng · Jiàn. Can't be in a chī (3-in-a-row).
02 · Win How you win胡hú
A standard winning hand = 4 melds + 1 duì = 14 tiles. You hold 13; draw or claim a 14th tile that completes your hand, then declare Hú.

03 · Turn What you can do招zhāo
On your turn — one move
Draw 摸mō · then discard

Take one tile from the wall — you now hold 14. Discard one face-up into the middle to return to 13.

  • New tile makes you win → declare Hú, game ends.
  • Complete 4 of one tile → option to declare a concealed gàng; if you do, reveal it and draw one bonus tile, then discard.
·
On any player's discard — every player watches

When the player whose turn it is discards a tile, any player may interrupt and claim it. Choose one — higher rank wins if multiple players want it:

① 胡
HúAnytime · any seat

The tile completes your winning hand (4 melds + 1 duì). Game ends.

② 杠
GàngAnytime · any seat

Take it to make four of a kind. Reveal the meld face-up and draw one bonus tile, then discard.

③ 碰
PèngAnytime · any seat

Take it to make three of a kind. Reveal the meld face-up, then discard.

④ 吃
ChīOnly from the player right before you

Take it to make 3 in a row, same suit. Reveal the meld face-up, then discard.

How play continues
  • No one claims → turn passes counter-clockwise to the next player on the discarder's right.
  • After Pèng / Gàng / Chī → claimer discards, then play continues with the player after them. Players in between are skipped.
  • After Hú → game ends.
  • Wall empties with no winner → game is a draw 荒莊huāng zhuāng.
Concealed vs. exposed

Melds you build from your own draws stay concealed in hand. Melds claimed from a discard sit face-up.
